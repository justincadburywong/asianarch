Tung T. Nguyen, MD, ARCH Director

I am a Professor of Clinical Medicine at UCSF, where I see patients, train health professionals, and conduct community-based participatory research (CBPR) to address cancer screening, tobacco control, and hepatitis B among Asian Americans. I am a Principal Investigator of the Asian American Network for Cancer Awareness, Research, and Training (AANCART) and Co-Leader of the UCSF Helen Diller Family Comprehensive Cancer Center’s Cancer Control Program. I also serve as a Commissioner on President Barack Obama's Advisory Commission on AAPIs.

Alka Kanaya, MD, ARCH Co-Director

I am a Professor of Medicine, Epidemiology & Biostatistics at UCSF, where I see patients in General Medicine practice, teach and mentor residents and fellows, and conduct patient-centered clinical research in the prevention of type 2 diabetes and cardiovascular disease specifically focusing on Asian American populations. I am the Principal Investigator for the MASALA (Mediators of Atherosclerosis in South Asians Living in America) study.
