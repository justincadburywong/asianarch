On October 30, 2012, we announced the establishment the new Asian American Research
 Center on Health (ARCH). Our members include prominent researchers, community leaders,
 and clinicians in the San Francisco Bay Area. The Centerwill maintain an open and
 collaborative environment to carry out the vision, which is   to be a leader in Asian
 American health research to promote health and health   equity in partnership with
 Asian American communities.
                                             Tung Nguyen, M.D., ARCH Director
                                             Alka Kanaya, M.D., ARCH Co-Director
